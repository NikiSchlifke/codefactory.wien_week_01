WWF

The World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) is an international organization working on issues regarding the conservation, research and restauration of the enviroment, formerly named the World Wildlife Fund. WWF was founded in 1961.

WWF's Panda symbol

The Panda has become the symbol of the WWF. The well-known logo of WWF originated from a panda named Chi Chi that was transfered from the Beijing Zoo to the London Zoo in the same year of the establishment of the WWF.
